What Are Search Notes?

When Swank cannot find your original search, Swank will run a related search based on a broader search conditions and listing types.

OSNF-AC

Original Search Not Found, Searched All Conditions for Results

OSNF-AC+ALT

Original Search Not Found, Searched All Conditions & All Listing Types for Results

Example

You search for a used item, that returns no swank results,
but when swank checks the includes new items along with the second search, you'll get back swank rank (+ OSNF-AC).
Even so, you would want to know that your used item had no results, but these new items will help you to estimate the market value of your used item
